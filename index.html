The Story of Dallas & Poirot

A journal of two detective kittens and their daily antics.

    The Family:
  • Michaela: the food giver and scratchies provider. She doesn't fully believe she's an adult quite yet, but she's doing her best for these two little floof balls.
  • Dallas: The smallest, but also the bravest and boldest. She hates to be picked up or moved against her will, but she'll love to the fullest once you win her trust.
  • Poirot: The craziest and the cuddliest. He literally runs up the walls at times, but he's also quick to run up and give a loving head butt to a new friend.
